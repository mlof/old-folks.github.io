What do you want to make?

Tools were in use on earth 3.4 million years ago (NewScientist, 2019). People (and our earliest relatives) have taken raw materials and joined them together in infinite ways for infinite uses for millions of years. Here are my tiny contributions to this culture of making. New ideas are in 'Do', ideas that I am working on (often slowly!) are in 'Doing' and things I have completed are in 'Done'. The final stage contains the projects I have documented.
